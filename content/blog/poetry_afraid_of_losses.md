I used to be afraid of losses.<br>

I always wanted to win and if I was going down,<br>

would go down swinging.<br>

Until I realized that going down,<br>
makes for the best fertilizer,<br>

to a great climax,<br>

of any story you’re telling.<br>

Gogan<br>
