Sometimes when I quote great men and women,<br>
I seem to inspire great and deep thoughts.<br>

And once in a while I will inspire a woman’s eyes to drool,<br>
when the light hits my good side just right.<br>

Turning them into shimmering saucers that contain,<br>

(and I’ve counted them,)<br>

every star in our galaxy.<br>

There must be something in the water.<br>

I’ve noticed when things get really w e t,<br>

I swear the entire earth shivers with delight.<br>

Was that just me?<br>

Or did you feel it too?<br>

Gogan<br>
