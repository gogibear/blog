I was chasing the other side of a magnet for so long,<br>

I could see it but couldn’t get there, it felt rare.<br>

And that energy was so strong it was addictive.<br>

It felt like a kept promise. The answer to my prayer.<br>

But it only ever pushed me away.<br>

And we know a tree by the fruit it bears.<br>

I don’t know why I kept expecting magnets to flip their poles.<br>

Maybe I knew the entire time the potential energy that<br>
existed there.<br>

But electricity takes the path of least resistance and that is its<br>
power. So I followed suit.<br>
Even though I usually tend to break them.<br>

And now through my eyes, this entire world looks like it’s in<br>
repair.<br>

Gogan<br>
