I was never happy as a pile of grain.<br>

It wasn’t until my hulls were cracked,<br>
and I was sifted while I drifted.<br>

And then ground into fine flour.<br>

Drowned in water.<br>

Burned by fire.<br>

That I became a loaf of bread that could feed any hunger,<br>
and satisfy any desire a heart could crave.<br>

It feels like I smiled for the first time today.<br>

Gogan<br>
