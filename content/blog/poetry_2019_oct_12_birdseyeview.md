This all feels like a bigger work.<br>

And each puzzle piece the sun hands me,<br>

might not have all its partners yet.<br>

But when I close my eyes,<br>

and feel my two feet on this earth,<br>

take a deep breath,<br>

to stoke the fire in my soul,<br>

and send the juice of life coursing through my veins.<br>

Everything decidedly already looks perfect,<br>

when I open them again,<br>

and look down from a million trillion light years away.<br>

Gogan<br>
