Neti neti.<br>

You add so,<br>

You’re left with,<br>

What is not it.<br>

Like a sculptor removes what is not,<br>

To reveal what is.<br>

Under the stone facade,<br>

A heart that blooms life, like an orchid.<br>

Gogan<br>
