I got jealous of what women can do,<br>

Now there’s a sun in my stomach too.<br>

If you ever see a migration,<br>

That would make monarchs seem common,<br>

You’ll know I’m busy at my life’s work,<br>

Dressing up as a brahmin.<br>

My sun is quite full of itself,<br>

With the gravity of a thousand black holes,<br>

It pulls in worries, fears and anxiety,<br>

And then it burns them whole.<br>

Come sit with me,<br>

And if you feel your aura cleaned,<br>

It’s not me it’s the fire.<br>

The only thing I’ve ever wanted,<br>

The boon of exploring all the depths that I’ve perceived.<br>

Gogan<br>
