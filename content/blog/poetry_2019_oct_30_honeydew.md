Not all doctors are healers.<br>

Not all teachers will educate you.<br>

Not all professors profess their truth.<br>

Not every coffee is perfectly brewed.<br>

The flower that could grow from the seed in you,<br>

Might not have a name yet. But honey do,<br>

you really think that’s a good reason not to bloom?<br>

Gogan<br>
