Sometimes when we are broken,<br>
we may need a crutch to help ourselves back on our feet.<br>
But some days when I look around,<br>
I see people collecting crutches,<br>
and showing them off like trophies.<br>

I used to look at myself and wonder what I was doing wrong,<br>
until I looked inside my torso and saw I'd collected and<br>
stacked every kind of crutch that's ever existed.<br>

So I reached into my pack for lightning that I had been<br>
saving for a special occasion,<br>
and set the pile ablaze.<br>

To my surprise the immense fire that now burned inside of me,<br>
had a mouth,<br>
and when it spoke, like frost on a winter's morning, smoke<br>
billowed out.<br>

It only ever said one thing,<br>

it promised that it would keep me warm for eternity.<br>

I'd like to invite you to sit around the campfire.<br>

And share with me any trouble or worry, I will wrap them up<br>
in graham crackers,<br>
and extend them into the flames.<br>

Pain, anxiety and stress are nothing more than the<br>
marshmallow,<br>
that will melt away into pure sweetness,<br>
in a perfectly cooked smore.<br>
