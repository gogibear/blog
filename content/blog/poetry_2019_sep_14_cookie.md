They brought me up to the right temperature.<br>

All the worries you sent my way.<br>
Otherwise I might have remained undone forever.<br>

Now like a cookie that can be chewy, soft, crunchy, crisp,<br>

savoury or sweet,<br>

depending on the person who takes a bite,<br>

all my Yelp reviews only say one thing anymore,<br>

"Mmm just the way I like it."<br>
