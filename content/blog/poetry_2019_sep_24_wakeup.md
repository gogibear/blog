I once visited a town,<br>
that I knew must be magic as soon as I arrived.<br>

Everyone there was always in perfect spirits,<br>

and I wondered what kind of spell they were under.<br>

So early one morning before dawn,<br>

and long before anyone was awake,<br>

I hid in the tallest building in town and waited to see what magician would appear.<br>

But all I saw were sad faces stumbling out of each home.<br>

Except one.<br>

A man who must have been in some great fire,<br>

because his face was burned into a permanent grin that may have looked grotesque,<br>
if you didn't know how it happened.<br>

He made his way to each person, gently raised their chin so their head was held high and whispered something in their ears.<br>

Luckily I'd made a cone with a piece of paper,<br>
so I raised it to my own and heard him say:<br>

"We need you today. We won't make it without you. Wake up."<br>

With all the evidence I needed I made my conclusion.<br>

He must have had a front row seat,<br>
when he saw the light.<br>
