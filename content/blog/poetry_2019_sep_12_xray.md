The gifts I give are like diamonds,<br>
that take root in your soul.<br>
And they mate and multiply for the rest of your life.<br>

Even though their street value is something like the GDP of Atlantis and Wakanda put together,<br>
Their real worth shines on those days when the light around you seems dim.<br>
Because these gems I share so freely have an uncountable number of facets.<br>

And each one reflects light into every corner of your being,<br>
and who can help but feel like a million bucks, when every inch of you is aglow with,<br>

usually I'd just write love here and call it a day,<br>

but today something tells me I should be more specific,<br>

so let's try that again:<br>

And who can help but feel like a million bucks, when every inch of you is aglow with,<br>

The feeling that you're worth it.<br>
