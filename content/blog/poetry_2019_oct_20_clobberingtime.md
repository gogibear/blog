Whenever people clobber me,<br>

With their words or their fists,<br>

I’ve realized they just have something to share with me.<br>

Sometimes I make angry eyebrows and engage with them for a<br>
bit,<br>

Until I show them I’m as crazy as they are,<br>

But then instead of biting out their throats I ask,<br>

What’s really bothering you?<br>

Gogan<br>
