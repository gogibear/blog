Sometimes anger will send it’s wasps my way,<br>

And their stingers hold me down to this place.<br>

Like pins in my existence.<br>

I gladly indulge once in a while.<br>

Otherwise I’d float out into that crystal nether,<br>

And there’s still so many flavours I have to taste.<br>

Gogan<br>
