Maybe we should go back to math class.<br>

It seems like we only learned joy division, no not the band.<br>
The division of labour from joy.<br>
We've made not enjoying ourselves very efficient.<br>
We've separated the juice from the fruit.<br>
The savouring of sweetness is in another department.<br>

Luckily I went on strike ages ago.<br>
I've got water in my bottle,<br>
and my pocket's full of sand.<br>

I've got the ingredients for a good time.<br>

You don't need to take me back,<br>
because I'm always at the beach.<br>

PS: You're always invited where I am.<br>
