My mom filled me up with all the kindling, and firewood I<br>
needed to turn into what I am today,<br>
People usually call all that material love.<br>

But then I had to go out into life and face some friction,<br>

To generate the sparks needed to catch on fire.<br>

And there were times I got absolutely drenched in fear,<br>

And was put out, or slowed down,<br>
And felt unworthy.<br>

As a matter of fact for a few years I was so head over heels in<br>
hate with myself,<br>

I thought about throwing it all away and going back to where<br>
we come from.<br>

I just couldn’t see it. What my mom saw.<br>

I only just realized we were mirrors for each other.<br>

Gogan<br>
