We could put all the blame in a bucket,<br>

And dole it equally all around.<br>

And no one would ever get full.<br>

And what would get done?<br>

You may as well trace rays of light back to the sun,<br>

And make diplomas for each explosion that was responsible.<br>
Sometimes when you are in a spot you want to get out of,<br>

You need to train your hands to turn every spear or splinter,<br>

That's ever been plunged into your tender flesh,<br>

Into the next rung of the ladder you use to get out.<br>
Regardless of who received the most votes,<br>

For spilling the milk.<br>

Gogan<br>
