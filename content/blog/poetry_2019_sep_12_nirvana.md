Sometimes you're Attached, when you want to feel Connected<br>

A grain of sand stayed up all night,<br>
worrying about the wellbeing of the desert.<br>
It clung to the grains all around it tightly as it could.<br>
I think in elementary school,<br>
or perhaps even earlier, someone had given it the silly notion<br>
that love was a kind of bondage.<br>

It wasn't until it ran out of strength and was forced to let go,<br>
that the grace of a passing wind took it in its fingers and<br>
raised it up high. Higher than heaven.<br>
The grain of sand looked down and for the first time lost<br>
sight of all its siblings,<br>
and caught a glimpse of what it was a part of.<br>

It hitched a ride back down in a droplet of water. In the<br>
morning the desert smiled.<br>
