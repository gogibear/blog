You don’t have to go far,<br>

To feel the soul of nature.<br>

Open up your door,<br>

This time of year is magic.<br>

All the colours of autumn,<br>

The shades and the hues,<br>

Will tell you only unadulterated truth.<br>

But you’re too busy to listen,<br>

While you try so hard to prove,<br>

All the things that actually come so naturally to you.<br>

The birds you hear and see,<br>

Can tell you more than any tweet.<br>

This morning was a beautiful reminder,<br>

That when I keep my heart open,<br>

The only thing I can think or say is,<br>

“Oh, this is such a feeling.”<br>

Gogan<br>
