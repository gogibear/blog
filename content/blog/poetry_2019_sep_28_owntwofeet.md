We could put all the blame in a bucket,<br>
And dole it equally all around.<br>

And no one would ever get full. And what would get done?<br>
You may as well trace rays of light back to the sun,<br>
And make diplomas for each explosion that was responsible.<br>

Sometimes when you are in a spot you want to get out of,<br>
You need to train your hands to turn every spear or splinter,<br>
That’s ever been plunged into your tender flesh,<br>
Into the next rung of the ladder you use to get out.<br>

Because when we are in a pit where hope seems dim,<br>
We may spend all our time tallying the benefit of spilling the<br>
beans,<br>
Or the cost of being accused of crying wolf.<br>

But the moment you get out into the light,<br>
It becomes so much easier to spot the devil in disguise,<br>
And point out to all those nearby to beware the poisoned<br>
chalice.<br>

So that instead of feeling like we need to accept Trojan<br>
horses,<br>
We know we will always be able to get to where we’re going,<br>
On our own two feet.<br>

Gogan<br>
