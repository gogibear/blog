I was trekking in unfamiliar terrain.<br>

Further there than I'd ever gotten before,<br>
I found myself in a hot, humid jungle,<br>

Searching for that thing that seems to be the reason for every movement we make.<br>

Sure that I was going in the right direction,<br>
hot on the scent of an exotic creature.<br>

In hindsight, maybe I was delirious.<br>

I heard the sound of a gun being loaded,<br>
and froze.<br>

I turned and saw what looked like the most beautiful light in the world, when I was hit with those 6 bullets that will kill or cripple any man,<br>

I. like. you. as. a. friend.<br>

Ouch. It seems like those bullets make wounds that don't heal cleanly if I can still describe them so well.<br>

On the bright side,<br>

Depending on how you connect the dots,<br>

You can draw every constellation in the sky<br>

On my now heavenly body.<br>
