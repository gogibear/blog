They all undress themselves,<br>

When they bark at me, every detail of their insecurity,<br>
Each syllable sheds another piece of protection,<br>

They'd put up between themselves and the world.<br>

I let them continue until they are stark naked.<br>

Their mighty visored helms are always the last to fall.<br>

But instead of plunging my sword straight through their heart,<br>

I angle it just so the light of the sun, can reignite the flame in their eyes that's been dead for so long.<br>
