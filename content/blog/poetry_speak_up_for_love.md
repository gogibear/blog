Every morning I wake up with this feeling that fills me up,<br>

from head to toe,<br>

That tells me to yell something interesting enough,<br>

That even the furthest stars would cup their ears to know.<br>

Maybe if I get good enough they’ll mistake me for Thoreau.<br>

It’s funny though,<br>

I didn’t learn the language of my head, my heart and my gut<br>
until recently,<br>

I remember misinterpreting the same message,<br>

From my head as,<br>

“You’re so far behind.”<br>

From my heart as,<br>

“No one will ever love you.”<br>

From my gut as,<br>

“There’s no way you can do this.”<br>

Ha!<br>

We’ve got to speak up for love. Where you are.<br>
With the words you have.<br>

You’re dying to let it out.<br>

Try living and let it go.<br>
Say hello. Let it flow.<br>

Before you know it you’ll be aglow.<br>
Don’t be shy and let it show.<br>

I swear you’ll feel like you were standing with every atom on<br>
earth,<br>
Under a giant mistletoe.<br>

Gogan<br>
