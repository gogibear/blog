With all our light we’ve obscured the stars,<br>

We’ve boycotted the Theater,<br>

That god so graciously built for us.<br>

The shooting stars riot in revolt.<br>

Thank heaven they’re only warning shots.<br>

I hope we smarten up soon.<br>

And understand that pointing a flashlight into your own<br>
eyes,<br>

typically isn’t its best use.<br>

Gogan<br>
