You can't see them yet,<br>
my new muscles,<br>

because they're still in repair here.<br>
Perhaps I look weaker than I ever have.<br>

I've torn the muscles in my body beyond all recognition,<br>
my heart especially got a raw deal,<br>
while I was on the path of the unseen. On the way from the unreal to real. The path where you leave no tracks. No trace. It's the same route birds take, where there are no footsteps to follow. Where you have to let go of enough to fly.<br>

But if you do gain what they call the third eye in India you might swear you were standing in front of the Incredible Hulk.<br>

If you don't have time for that you can use my writing as a pair of glasses that will shade everything you see the colour of reality.<br>
